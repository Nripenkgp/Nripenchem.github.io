Research Summary

Computational chemist specializing in DFT, TDDFT, energy decomposition analysis (EDA), and mechanistic studies. Experienced in modeling CO2 reduction, electron and charge transfer, and main group catalysis. Skilled in Gaussian16, ORCA, ADF, Quantum ESPRESSO, and HPC cluster computing.

Research Experience

  • Doctoral Research Intern — IISER Kolkata (2022–2027)

    Computational investigation of the Chan-Lam coupling reaction and small molecule activation by main-group catalysts using DFT/TDDFT. Developed multi-layer ONIOM models and mechanistic analysis workflows.

  • M.Sc Research Intern — IIT Kharagpur (2021–2022)

    EDA and QTAIM analyses of LMCN/NC complexes and isomerization pathways.

  • Summer Research Intern — IIT Kharagpur (2021)

    Machine learning for global optimization of atomic clusters; Gaussian16 interfaced Python workflows.
